Top 3 Famous Crypto Currency

Name Famous Crypto Currency Description
Bitcoin Bitcoin, introduced in 2009 by Satoshi Nakamoto, is a virtual currency designed to act as money and payment without third-party involvement. It has become the world's largest cryptocurrency and inspired the development of numerous other cryptocurrencies.
ZCash ZCash is a cryptocurrency and blockchain that was created in 2016 when a group of academics decided they wanted to make a cryptocurrency that was similar to Bitcoin but had a few more characteristics. They created a Bitcoin blockchain split that offers improved privacy and security for users. Zerocoin, which was first created by the scientists, soon changed its name to Zerocash. The cryptocurrency was eventually rebranded as ZCash.
Litecoin Litecoin (LTC) is a cryptocurrency that emerged from a fork of the Bitcoin network in 2011. It was originally intended to solve the developer's fears that Bitcoin was becoming too centrally controlled and make it more difficult for large-scale mining businesses to obtain an advantage in mining. While the cryptocurrency was ultimately unsuccessful in keeping commercial miners from controlling the majority of Litecoin mining, it has evolved into a minable coin and a peer-to-peer payment system.
